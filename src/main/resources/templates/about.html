About MyWelly

Our Mission

MyWelly is Morocco's leading healthcare booking platform, dedicated to transforming how Moroccans access healthcare services. We connect patients with verified doctors and laboratories across the country, making quality healthcare accessible to everyone.

Our mission is to simplify the healthcare journey by providing a seamless, transparent, and trustworthy platform where patients can find, compare, and book appointments with healthcare providers at their convenience.

What We Offer

For Patients

  • Search and compare doctors by specialty, location, and price
  • Book appointments 24/7 with real-time availability
  • Read verified reviews from real patients
  • Access detailed doctor profiles with qualifications and experience
  • Manage all appointments in one place
  • Find nearby laboratories for medical tests

For Healthcare Providers

  • Increase visibility and reach more patients
  • Manage appointments efficiently
  • Build your online reputation through reviews
  • Streamline practice administration
  • Connect with patients across Morocco
